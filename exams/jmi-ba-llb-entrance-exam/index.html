JMI BA LLB Syllabus — Section by Section

Numerical Ability

  • Number system & simplification
  • Percentage, profit & loss
  • Ratio, proportion, averages & mixtures
  • Time & work
  • Simple interest
  • Number series & fractions

General English

  • Synonyms & antonyms
  • One-word substitution
  • Sentence completion (vocabulary)
  • Idioms & usage

General Awareness & Current Affairs

  • National & international current affairs
  • Awards & honours
  • Sports
  • Books & authors
  • Government schemes & appointments

General Studies

  • History
  • Geography
  • Indian polity
  • General science
  • Economy
  • Static GK

Legal Aptitude

  • Legal terms, maxims & foreign phrases
  • Constitutional & legal general knowledge
  • Principle–fact legal reasoning (contracts, torts, fundamental rights)

Eligibility for JMI BA LLB Entrance Exam

  • Passed the 10+2 (Senior Secondary) examination, or equivalent, with the minimum aggregate marks specified in the JMI prospectus.
  • Candidates appearing in the qualifying examination may apply subject to meeting the eligibility before admission.
  • Refer to the official Jamia Millia Islamia admission notification for the exact eligibility and age criteria for the 5-year integrated BA LLB (Hons.) programme.

How to Apply

Mode: Online only — there is no offline/paper form.

Application fee: ₹700 per programme (₹1,000 for some self-financed programmes) — non-refundable, paid online. Confirm the exact amount for your course on the portal.

  1. Register on admission.jmi.ac.in with your name, a working email and mobile number.
  2. Fill the application form — personal details, qualifying-exam marks, and select BA LLB (Hons.) as your programme.
  3. Upload a scanned photograph and signature in the prescribed format and size.
  4. Pay the application fee online (UPI, debit/credit card or net banking).
  5. Download and save the confirmation page — you will need it at the time of admission.
