JMI MA Mass Media (Hindi) Exam Pattern

Postgraduate admission test for the AJK Mass Communication Research Centre (MCRC) — Part A is an objective MCQ paper; Part B is descriptive writing · Medium: Hindi

SectionQuestionsMarks
Part A — Objective MCQs (media GK, current affairs, polity, history, Hindi language & literature, general knowledge)4040
Part B — Descriptive Writing (marked separately; not part of the online mock)—60
Total — 90 minutes40100

Marking scheme: +1 for each correct MCQ in Part A (40 marks); −0.25 for each wrong answer. Part B (descriptive, 60 marks) is marked separately.

Pattern is taken from the official instruction page of the digitised papers (Paper Code M-85). Part A carries 40 one-mark MCQs with −0.25 negative marking; Part B is descriptive writing worth 60 marks. The JamiaPrep mock reproduces only the objective Part A. A few source items with no determinable correct answer have been omitted. Always confirm the current pattern with the official JMI / AJK MCRC admission notification.

JMI MA Mass Media (Hindi) Syllabus — Section by Section

Media & Journalism

  • History of Indian press & journalism
  • Newspapers, magazines & their editors
  • Radio, television & OTT/digital media
  • Communication models & media theory
  • News agencies & press laws

Current Affairs

  • National & international current affairs
  • Awards, honours & appointments
  • Sports & important events
  • Government schemes & days

Indian Polity & History

  • Indian Constitution & polity
  • Modern Indian history & the national movement
  • Art, culture & geography

Hindi Language & Literature

  • Hindi authors, poets & their works
  • Hindi grammar (vyakaran)
  • Literary magazines & movements
  • Idioms & vocabulary

General Knowledge

  • Static GK
  • Science & technology awareness
  • Economy & banking basics
  • Books & authors

How to Apply

Mode: Online only — there is no offline/paper form.

Application fee: ₹700 per programme (₹1,000 for some self-financed programmes) — non-refundable, paid online. Confirm the exact amount for your course on the portal.

  1. Register on admission.jmi.ac.in with your name, a working email and mobile number.
  2. Fill the application form — personal details, qualifying-exam marks, and select MA Mass Media (Hindi) as your programme.
  3. Upload a scanned photograph and signature in the prescribed format and size.
  4. Pay the application fee online (UPI, debit/credit card or net banking).
  5. Download and save the confirmation page — you will need it at the time of admission.
