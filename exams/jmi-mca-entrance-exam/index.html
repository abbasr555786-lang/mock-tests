JMI MCA Syllabus — Section by Section

Computer Concepts & Programming

  • Computer fundamentals & organization
  • Number systems & digital logic
  • C programming (operators, functions, files, strings)
  • Data structures & algorithm basics
  • Operating systems & system software
  • Networks & internet basics

Mathematics

  • Sets, relations & functions
  • Algebra & quadratic equations
  • Matrices & determinants
  • Limits, continuity & differentiation
  • Integration & differential equations
  • Permutations, combinations & probability
  • Logarithms & binomial theorem

Reasoning

  • Blood relations
  • Coding–decoding
  • Syllogisms
  • Non-verbal figure series

English

  • Tenses & voice
  • Prepositions & conjunctions
  • Synonyms & vocabulary
  • Punctuation & sentence types

Eligibility for JMI MCA Entrance Exam

  • Bachelor's degree (minimum 3 years) with Mathematics at 10+2 or graduation level, with the minimum aggregate marks specified in the JMI prospectus.
  • Final-year graduation candidates may apply subject to meeting the eligibility before admission.
  • Refer to the official Jamia Millia Islamia admission notification for the exact eligibility for the MCA programme.

How to Apply

Mode: Online only — there is no offline/paper form.

Application fee: ₹700 per programme (₹1,000 for some self-financed programmes) — non-refundable, paid online. Confirm the exact amount for your course on the portal.

  1. Register on admission.jmi.ac.in with your name, a working email and mobile number.
  2. Fill the application form — personal details, qualifying-exam marks, and select MCA as your programme.
  3. Upload a scanned photograph and signature in the prescribed format and size.
  4. Pay the application fee online (UPI, debit/credit card or net banking).
  5. Download and save the confirmation page — you will need it at the time of admission.
