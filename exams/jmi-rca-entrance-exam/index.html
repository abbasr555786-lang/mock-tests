JMI RCA Exam Pattern

Screening test for the Residential Coaching Academy — Section A objective MCQs (general studies, current affairs, reasoning & comprehension) + Section B essay writing · Medium: Trilingual — English, Hindi & Urdu (the English version is authoritative in case of any difference)

SectionQuestionsMarks
Section A — Objective MCQs (general studies, current affairs, polity, history, geography, economy, reasoning & comprehension)100100
Section B — Essay Writing (marked separately; not part of the online mock)—60
Total — 180 minutes100160

Marking scheme: +1 for each correct MCQ in Section A; one-third (1/3) of a mark is deducted for each wrong answer. Section B (essay) is marked separately.

Pattern is taken from the official instruction pages of the digitised papers. Section A is objective and Section B is essay writing (60 marks). The split varies by cycle: in 2023-24 and 2024-25, Section A had 100 MCQs in 2 hours (Section B essay in 1 hour, 160 marks total); in 2021-22 and 2022-23, Section A had 60 MCQs in 1 hour (two essays in 2 hours, 120 marks total). The JamiaPrep mock reproduces only the objective Section A. The RCA test is for free residential coaching for the UPSC Civil Services Examination, reserved for SC/ST, OBC (non-creamy), minority and women candidates — always confirm the current pattern with the official RCA / JMI notification.

JMI RCA Syllabus — Section by Section

General Studies & Current Affairs

  • National & international current affairs
  • Government schemes & flagship programmes
  • Banking, economy & labour statistics
  • Science & technology, defence and space
  • Environment & ecology

Indian Polity

  • Constitution, Fundamental Rights & Directive Principles
  • Union & State executive (President, Vice-President, Governor)
  • Emergency provisions
  • Panchayati Raj & State Finance Commission
  • Constitutional & statutory bodies

History & Culture

  • Ancient India (Harappa, Vedic & Upanishadic thought, Buddhism & Jainism)
  • Medieval India (guilds, south Indian polity)
  • Modern India & the national movement (Khilafat, Congress ministries, Tipu Sultan)

Geography

  • Physical features & mountain passes
  • Ocean currents & climate / jet streams
  • Census & population
  • Minerals, mines & ports

Reasoning & Quantitative Aptitude

  • Coding-decoding & number transformation
  • Syllogisms
  • Clocks & calendars
  • Sets, pigeonhole & arithmetic puzzles

Reading Comprehension

  • Inference & main-idea questions
  • Author's tone & attitude
  • Literary/critical passages

Eligibility for JMI RCA Entrance Exam

  • Open to candidates from SC/ST, OBC (non-creamy layer), notified minority communities and women who aspire to the UPSC Civil Services Examination.
  • Must hold a graduation degree (or be appearing in the final year) and meet the age limit prescribed for the UPSC Civil Services Examination.
  • Refer to the official Jamia Millia Islamia Residential Coaching Academy notification for the exact category, age and income criteria for the given cycle.

How to Apply

Mode: Online only — there is no offline/paper form.

Application fee: ₹700 per programme (₹1,000 for some self-financed programmes) — non-refundable, paid online. Confirm the exact amount for your course on the portal.

  1. Register on admission.jmi.ac.in with your name, a working email and mobile number.
  2. Fill the application form — personal details, qualifying-exam marks, and select RCA Civil Services (IAS/IPS) coaching as your programme.
  3. Upload a scanned photograph and signature in the prescribed format and size.
  4. Pay the application fee online (UPI, debit/credit card or net banking).
  5. Download and save the confirmation page — you will need it at the time of admission.
